Dating Securely

Tips to be safe

  1. Never send any money to a lady.
  2. Exchange contact information with the lady through “Call me” service.
  3. Once in a while conduct video chat with your lady.

Scam and reimbursements

  1. A lady misidentifies herself. (For example, an agency uses her profile without her permission to communicate with you). We reimburse all credits you incurred communicating with the lady.
  2. A lady requests money or expensive gifts from you. We reimburse all credits spent on communicating with the lady and ban her permanently.
  3. A lady deliberately misrepresents her details: age, kids, gender, marital status or language level. We reimburse all credits you incurred communicating with the lady and ban her permanently.
  4. Gifts of flowers that were ordered through the system were not delivered. We reimburse all credits you incurred for the flower delivery order.

Not scam

  1. The agency makes a mistake in the translation of the letter.
  2. If a lady doesn’t answer all questions in an email.
  3. If after meeting with customer she says she is not interested.
  4. If a lady has photos of her relatives, male friends, ex-boyfriend, ex-husband and other personal info posted on any social network.
  5. If a lady wears rings on fingers. Wearing rings in Latin countries does not mean a woman is married, regardless of which finger it is on.
